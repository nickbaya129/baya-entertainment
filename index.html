Founder

Nick Baya

I’ve spent over 15 years in the events, wedding, and luxury entertainment industry — performing at more than 1,300 events globally across venues, destination weddings, and yachts.

In most of these events, I’ve been directly involved in setting up and working within the technical environment — from sound systems and lighting to layout and positioning. Over time, I started to notice the same issues repeatedly: great talent being held back by poor setup, inconsistent sound, weak lighting, and a lack of overall flow.

This wasn’t isolated to one region — I saw the same problems across the UK, Europe, and internationally. And in many cases, small adjustments on the day would completely transform the outcome of an event.

BAYA Entertainment was created to bring that knowledge into a dedicated consultancy — helping clients design events properly from the ground up, avoid common mistakes, and consistently deliver a high-end experience.

What We Do

Technical clarity. Seamless guest experience.

BAYA Entertainment specialises in the technical and experiential design of luxury events. From sound systems and lighting to layout, performance positioning, and overall flow, we make sure the setup supports the atmosphere the event is meant to deliver.

We work with weddings, private events, venues, and yachts that want a more considered, elevated, and consistent experience.

The Problem

Great talent can still be held back by the setup.

  • Poor sound coverage
  • Incorrect layout and positioning
  • Lighting that feels flat or mismatched
  • No structured flow to the night

Result: events that fall short of their potential.

The Solution

We bridge creative vision and technical execution.

  • Correct system specification
  • Strategic layout design
  • Lighting that enhances atmosphere
  • Structured event flow and energy

The result is an event that feels effortless, cohesive, and high-end.
